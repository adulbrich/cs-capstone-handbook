# Project Handoff - Rubrics

## Project overview and status (12 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (12 to >9.6 pts): Clear summary of what was built and for whom; states current deployment or delivery state (is it running, where, how stable); lists any exclusions from the handoff and explains why; includes contact information for each team member.
- **Meets Expectations** (9.6 to >2.4 pts): Overview is present but deployment status is vague, exclusions are not addressed, or contact information is missing.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (2.4 to >0 pts): Overview is missing or too brief to orient someone new to the project.

## Setup and getting started: correctness and completeness (28 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (28 to >22.4 pts): Step-by-step instructions for getting the project running from scratch; lists all prerequisites (OS, runtime versions, tools, accounts); provides copy-pasteable commands for install, build, test, and local dev; covers how to deploy or links to a deployment runbook; includes common pitfalls and resolutions.
- **Meets Expectations** (22.4 to >5.6 pts): Setup instructions are present and mostly complete but missing some prerequisites, commands, or steps a new person would need.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (5.6 to >0 pts): Setup instructions are missing, incomplete, or inaccurate enough that they would not reliably work for a new person.

## Known issues, limitations, and future work (18 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (18 to >14.4 pts): Honest, prioritized list of open bugs (with tracker links or explicit list and severity), known limitations, identified technical debt, and a prioritized future work backlog; includes any credentials, secrets, or access permissions that need to be transferred; tone is honest, not promotional.
- **Meets Expectations** (14.4 to >3.6 pts): Known issues and future work are documented but prioritization is absent, severity is missing, or credential/access transfer is not addressed.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (3.6 to >0 pts): Section is missing or claims &ldquo;no known issues&rdquo; without evidence.

## Reference documents (8 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (8 to >6.4 pts): Final requirements document and final technical design document are linked or attached; links are accessible and clearly labeled.
- **Meets Expectations** (6.4 to >1.6 pts): One of the two documents is present, or both are linked but inaccessible or unlabeled.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.6 to >0 pts): Both documents are absent or inaccessible.

## Handoff checklist and partner verification (14 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (14 to >11.2 pts): Checklist is complete; all applicable items are checked or include a clear explanation and follow-up plan; written confirmation from the project partner (or instructor/mentor substitute) is included and demonstrates that they can access the repository, run the project, and understand future maintenance needs.
- **Meets Expectations** (11.2 to >2.8 pts): Checklist is included and mostly complete; partner confirmation is present but partial; some unchecked items lack explanation.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (2.8 to >0 pts): Checklist is missing; partner confirmation is absent; or nearly all items are unchecked without explanation.

## Course archive and release consent (15 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (15 to >12.0 pts): Archive drop link is submitted; archive contains all significant artifacts from all three terms (requirements, design, ADRs, test plans, reports, presentations, meeting notes, demo videos, source code, and any other course artifact); a release consent declaration is included in the archive with every team member marked as consenting or not consenting.
- **Meets Expectations** (12.0 to >3.0 pts): Archive is submitted but missing some artifact categories; or release consent declaration is present but not all team members are accounted for.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (3.0 to >0 pts): Archive is missing, inaccessible, or clearly incomplete; release consent declaration is absent.

## Writing and accessibility (5 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (5 to >4.0 pts): Professional tone throughout; instructions are precise and unambiguous; code blocks used for all commands and file paths; all links are descriptive and working; screenshots and diagrams include alt text and captions.
- **Meets Expectations** (4.0 to >1.0 pts): Mostly clear; minor formatting issues or broken links; minor gaps in code block usage or alt text.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): Unclear or unprofessional writing; missing code blocks; broken links; no alt text.
