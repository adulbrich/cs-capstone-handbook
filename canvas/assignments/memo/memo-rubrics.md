# Memo - Rubrics

## Interview planning and data capture (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): Plan includes goals and questions; memo shows 5–10 quotes/time-stamped notes; consent/recording status noted.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Plan or notes provided; includes ≥5 paraphrases/quotes.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): Insufficient planning/notes; <5 usable quotes/paraphrases.

## Problem understanding and constraints (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): Clarified problem statement plus 3+ constraints/acceptance criteria synthesized from interview.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Problem statement and at least 1–2 constraints identified.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): Problem statement unclear or lacks concrete constraints.

## IP/licensing and data-use considerations (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): Identifies IP ownership, license(s), and data privacy/security implications with next steps.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Notes basic IP/licensing or data considerations.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): Missing or incorrect IP/licensing/data considerations.

## Stakeholder expectations and professionalism (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): Explicit expectations captured (grading, SLAs, deliverables, comms cadence); confirms with stakeholder.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Expectations listed but not fully verified.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): Expectations unclear or not documented.

## Memo clarity and structure (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): 1 page, scannable headings/bullets; links work; no grammar barriers.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Understandable with minor issues or minor overlength.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): Hard to follow; missing sections or major grammar barriers.
