# ADR & Code Review - Rubrics

## ADR completeness and rigor (35 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (35 to >28.0 pts): States context, options (incl. 'do nothing'), decision, and consequences with rationale and tradeoffs.
- **Meets Expectations** (28 to >7.0 pts): States decision with some rationale and options.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (7 to >0 pts): Decision unclear; options/rationale missing.

## Code review quality (35 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (35 to >28.0 pts): Findings are actionable with examples/links; checks correctness, design, tests, security.
- **Meets Expectations** (28 to >7.0 pts): Provides general suggestions; addresses at least one area.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (7 to >0 pts): Superficial 'LGTM' (Looks Good To Me) or nit-only review.

## Adoption and follow-through (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): Evidence of updates from review or ADR (PR diffs/comments).
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Acknowledged; partial adoption.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): No follow-up evidence.

## Professional tone and standards (10 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (10 to >8.0 pts): Respectful, standards-referenced comments; links to docs.
- **Meets Expectations** (8 to >2.0 pts): Professional but brief.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (2 to >0 pts): Unprofessional tone or off-topic comments.
