# Progress Report - Rubrics

## Progress Against Plan (25 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (25 to >20.0 pts): Completed vs. planned work is clearly quantified; blockers and replans are documented; every claim links to issues/PRs/commits.
- **Meets Expectations** (20 to >5.0 pts): Accomplishments and next steps listed; some links provided; some quantification.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (5 to >0 pts): Updates vague; few or no links; no connection to plan.

## Evidence of Working Software (25 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (25 to >20.0 pts): Includes at least one of deploy links, CI/build status (badges/links), brief test summaries w/ metrics, 1–3 screenshots, or ≤2‑min clips. Commit hash(es) included; one artifact may demonstrate all completed changes.
- **Meets Expectations** (20 to >5.0 pts): Partial evidence of working software.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (5 to >0 pts): No evidence of working software.

## Risk & Quality Tracking (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): New risks logged; each risk has an owner and status; bug/test metrics show trend; clear actions assigned.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Risks/bugs mentioned; basic actions noted; limited ownership or trends.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): Risks/bugs not tracked, ownerless, or outdated.

## Writing Quality & Clarity (15 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (15 to >12.0 pts): Concise, well-structured, polished; uses headings/bullets; accessible formatting (alt text, captions); professional tone.
- **Meets Expectations** (12 to >3.0 pts): Mostly clear; some structure; minor clarity/formatting issues.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (3 to >0 pts): Hard to read; poorly structured; unclear or unprofessional tone.

## Team Process Reflection (15 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (15 to >12.0 pts): Clear retro with 1–2 actionable improvements AND evidence the team is applying them next sprint.
- **Meets Expectations** (12 to >3.0 pts): Reflection present with intended improvements.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (3 to >0 pts): No reflection or no actionable improvement items.
