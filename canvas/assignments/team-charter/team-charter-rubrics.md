# Team Charter - Rubrics

## Roles: clarity and responsibilities (8 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (8 to >6.5 pts): Roles are explicit, unambiguous, and cover key responsibilities; a new member could understand who does what.
- **Meets Expectations** (6.5 to >1.5 pts): Roles are defined but have minor gaps or overlap.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.5 to >0 pts): Roles are missing or too vague to be useful.

## Decision-making process (6 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (6 to >4.8 pts): Decision model is clear (how decisions are proposed, debated, and finalized) with at least one explicit escalation/override rule.
- **Meets Expectations** (4.8 to >1.0 pts): Decision process exists but is incomplete or lightly specified.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): No clear decision-making process.

## Risk management and escalation paths (6 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (6 to >4.8 pts): Key team risks are identified with clear escalation paths and triggers.
- **Meets Expectations** (4.8 to >1.0 pts): Risks are listed with basic escalation guidance.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): Risks and/or escalation paths are missing.

## Conflict resolution: steps and tone (8 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (8 to >6.5 pts): Conflict plan is restorative, time-bound, and includes clear steps from informal resolution to escalation.
- **Meets Expectations** (6.5 to >1.5 pts): Conflict plan is present with reasonable steps; escalation exists.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.5 to >0 pts): Conflict plan is missing, unclear, or punitive.

## Conflict resolution: measurable triggers (6 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (6 to >4.8 pts): Includes measurable triggers (e.g., missed meetings, unreviewed PR backlog) for when to escalate.
- **Meets Expectations** (4.8 to >1.0 pts): Triggers exist but are vague.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): No triggers for escalation.

## Accountability: evidence-based expectations (6 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (6 to >4.8 pts): Accountability is tied to observable evidence (e.g., PRs, reviews, attendance/notes) rather than vague promises.
- **Meets Expectations** (4.8 to >1.0 pts): Accountability is described but evidence ties are partial.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): No clear accountability expectations.

## Definition of Done: required checks (8 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (8 to >6.5 pts): DoD includes specific checks for tests, code review, documentation, and relevant quality/security/static analysis.
- **Meets Expectations** (6.5 to >1.5 pts): DoD includes tests and review; some checks are missing or underspecified.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.5 to >0 pts): DoD absent or generic.

## Definition of Done: enforcement evidence (6 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (6 to >4.8 pts): Evidence (links/logs/screenshots) shows DoD is actually enforced (e.g., CI gates, review rules).
- **Meets Expectations** (4.8 to >1.0 pts): Mentions enforcement, but evidence is limited.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): No enforcement or evidence.

## Contribution guidelines: CONTRIBUTING completeness (6 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (6 to >4.8 pts): `CONTRIBUTING.md` is comprehensive, references the DoD, and includes local commands for CI/linting/testing.
- **Meets Expectations** (4.8 to >1.0 pts): `CONTRIBUTING.md` exists but lacks detail or missing commands.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): `CONTRIBUTING.md` missing or empty.

## Communication: cadence and tools (5 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (5 to >4.0 pts): Meeting cadence and communication tools/channels are clearly stated, including where decisions are documented.
- **Meets Expectations** (4 to >1.0 pts): Cadence/tools stated with minor ambiguity.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): Communication plan unclear or missing.

## Inclusion: meeting norms and turn-taking (5 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (5 to >4.0 pts): Norms explicitly accommodate time zones, accessibility needs, and inclusive participation.
- **Meets Expectations** (4 to >1.0 pts): Inclusion norms present but general.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): No evidence of inclusive norms.

## Inclusion: accommodations process (5 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (5 to >4.0 pts): Clear process for requesting accommodations and how the team responds.
- **Meets Expectations** (4 to >1.0 pts): Openness to accommodations stated but process is light.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): No accommodations process.

## Change log: present and usable (5 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (5 to >4.0 pts): Change log is present and follows the required format.
- **Meets Expectations** (4 to >1.0 pts): Change log is present but inconsistent.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): Change log missing.

## Policies: owners and review dates (5 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (5 to >4.0 pts): Each policy includes an owner and review date.
- **Meets Expectations** (4 to >1.0 pts): Most policies have owners/dates.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): Owners/dates missing.

## Writing quality: structure and links (5 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (5 to >4.0 pts): Document is well-structured with clear headings and actionable bullets; links are functional.
- **Meets Expectations** (4 to >1.0 pts): Readable document with minor formatting issues.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): Hard to follow or unstructured.

## Individual contribution: verifiable evidence (6 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (6 to >4.8 pts): Clear, verifiable evidence of contribution (PRs, meeting records, etc.) provided in submission notes.
- **Meets Expectations** (4.8 to >1.0 pts): Evidence exists but is limited in detail.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (1.0 to >0 pts): No evidence provided.

## Individual contribution: meaningful engagement (4 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (4 to >3.2 pts): Evidence indicates meaningful engagement (not just minor edits), aligned to team needs.
- **Meets Expectations** (3.2 to >0.8 pts): Some engagement shown; impact unclear.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (0.8 to >0 pts): Evidence does not demonstrate participation.
