# Team Charter - Rubrics

## Clarity & Completeness of Roles/Processes (25 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (25 to >20.0 pts): Roles, decision rules, cadence, and tools are explicit, unambiguous, and cover all phases; risks and escalation paths included.
- **Meets Expectations** (20 to >5.0 pts): Defines roles, decisions, cadence, and tools with enough detail to run; minor gaps.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (5 to >0 pts): Missing key elements or vague; cannot guide team operations.

## Conflict Resolution & Accountability (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): Conflict plan includes measurable triggers, restorative steps, and escalation timeline; accountability tied to evidence (e.g., PRs, attendance).
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Conflict plan present with clear steps and escalation; accountability described at a high level.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): No clear conflict plan or accountability; punitive or unclear steps.

## Definition of Done & Quality Gates (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): DoD includes tests, code review, security checks, and documentation; CI gates named.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): DoD lists tests and review; basic CI gates referenced.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): DoD absent or only output-focused; no quality gates.

## Accessibility & Inclusivity Practices (15 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (15 to >12.0 pts): Meeting norms accommodate time zones, accessibility needs, and inclusive turn-taking; notes on accommodations.
- **Meets Expectations** (12 to >3.0 pts): States inclusive meeting norms and openness to accommodations.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (3 to >0 pts): No evidence of inclusive practices; potential barriers unaddressed.

## Professional Writing & Organization (20 pts)

- **Exceeds Expectations** (20 to >16.0 pts): Well-structured document with headings, actionable bullets, and owner+date on each policy.
- **Meets Expectations** (16 to >4.0 pts): Readable document with headings and action items.
- **Does Not Meet Expectations** (4 to >0 pts): Hard to follow; missing structure or owners/dates.
